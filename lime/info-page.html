What can you
do with Lime?

artists
As a verified artist you can have your own store within our app, release exclusive tracks, sell tickets and merchandise items and connect with your followers easier than ever before.
fans
For music lovers, we have put together a set of fun features that will make your interaction with the artists and your friends intuitive and exciting. You can play games with your friends within the chat, be in control of your music from any screen of the app and support your favorite artists.

We are always listening to what our community has to say so, if you think you have a great idea, create a thread in the forum, gather votes and see it become a feature in our app.

Let’s keep it
FREE

Every feature and element of Lime has been carefully considered and created in order to provide the best experience for you. We wanted a mobile app that does more than just play music, so we decided to create a full experience around it. Why? Because we believe that you deserve the best we can offer, and because we are perfectionists.

If you wanted to do all the things we offer with Lime, you would need at least 3 apps out of which you most likely would’ve had to pay for at least one. We want to keep the app free for everyone, and that is why we need your help!
